retext-sentiment — Detect the sentiment of text with Retext

A quick demo of wooorm/retext-sentiment.

Just paste (or write) some text in the text area below. I recommend Googling IMDb for reviews.

Please raise any issues on GitHub.

The positivity or negativity on paragraphs and sentences is depicted by their background colour. The polarity of words is depicted through a coloured underline. Pure red or green underlines depict maximum polarity; the more blue in the underline, the less extreme the “emotions”.
